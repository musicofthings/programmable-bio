Customer Value Proposition

Different customer segments have fundamentally different pain points. The value proposition must be segment-specific, not generic.

Indian Generic Pharma

Sun, Dr. Reddy's, Cipla, Lupin

Pain: Patent cliffs accelerating, need innovation pipelines not just generics.
Our value: AI-powered drug repurposing, biomarker-driven formulation science, NCE discovery at 1/5th the cost of US CROs. Regulatory dossiers India-ready.
Typical deal: $1–3M discovery sprint + LabOS sidecar.

MNC Pharma India R&D

Novartis, AZ, Pfizer, Roche

Pain: Need APAC population diversity in discovery; India data siloed and underutilized.
Our value: Atlas access, stratified trial design, faster IND-enabling packages.
Typical deal: Atlas license + IVD sprint + clinical platform (2026+).

Indian Biotech Startups

CAR-T, gene therapy, biologics

Pain: Can't afford full computational biology + wet lab teams.
Our value: Discovery-as-a-Service — pay-per-program, hypothesis to validated lead.
Typical deal: $500K–2M milestone sprint.

Hospital Research Arms

Apollo, Fortis, AIIMS, CMC

Pain: Rich clinical data, no AI-multiomics capability to convert to IP.
Our value: Clinical data → multiomics → biomarker pipeline. Co-IP + revenue share.
Typical deal: Co-development + Atlas co-build.

Government / Institutional

BIRAC, DBT, ICMR, IITs

Pain: Grant-funded research needs commercial translation.
Our value: Translational platform, co-IP, BIRAC co-application partner.
Typical deal: Grant + equity co-dev.

APAC Expansion Targets

Singapore, Japan, South Korea

Pain: Need APAC platform plays and diverse population datasets.
Our value: Pan-APAC discovery network, Singapore regulatory bridge.
Typical deal: BioHelix corridor program — see BioHelix.
