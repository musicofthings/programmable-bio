Pre-clinical — Organoids & 3D Cultures

Replacing animal testing with New Approach Methodologies (NAMs). The regulatory tailwind is real: the FDA Modernization Act 2.0 (December 2022) eliminated mandatory animal testing for new drugs; CDSCO is following with the Drugs and Cosmetics Amendment Rules. The science is also real: patient-derived organoids predict clinical response with higher accuracy than rodent models in oncology, IBD, and metabolic disease.

What we offer

Apollo patient-derived organoids

Tumor, intestinal, liver, kidney, and lung organoids derived from consented Apollo patients. Each organoid line ships with linked clinical phenotype, genomic data, and longitudinal treatment-response context. Irreplaceable for India-specific drug development.

3D cell cultures & spheroids

High-throughput-compatible 3D culture formats for efficacy and toxicity screening. Compatible with the LabOS Execution Broker for parallel screening across partner CRO sites.

Organ-on-chip & co-culture

Microfluidic models for liver, kidney, gut-blood-barrier, and tumor-immune microenvironment. Partnership-grade access to Emulate, MIMETAS, and TissUse platforms.

Patient-response prediction

Drug screens across panels of patient-derived organoids, with response data fed back into the OmicsOS Learning Agent to refine response-predictive biomarkers.

Where organoids beat animals

Use caseWhy organoids win
Oncology efficacyPatient-derived tumor organoids correlate with clinical response (~85% concordance) vs. xenografts (~50%)
HepatotoxicityLiver organoids capture human-specific metabolism (CYP variants) that rodents do not
Drug repositioningRapid screening across patient organoid panels surfaces unexpected indications faster than animal panels
Rare diseasePatient-derived organoids from N=1 patients enable mechanism work where no animal model exists

Engagement

$200K–$1M per program. Typical sprint: 6–9 months from biospecimen consent through drug screen completion and biomarker report. Bundled discounts when paired with a Target Discovery or Drug Discovery sprint.
